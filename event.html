Join our exclusive roster of brand ambassadors

We are constantly seeking new faces to represent the elegance and sophistication of the Christian Wijaya brand. If you embody our aesthetic and values, we invite you to apply to become part of our modeling family.

  • Age: At least 18 years old.

  • Availability for castings, fittings, and events in Jakarta
  • Previous modeling experience preferred but not required
