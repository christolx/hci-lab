Our Story

The legacy of craftsmanship and elegance

Our Heritage

Founded in 1992 by master craftsman Christian Wijaya, our luxury brand was born from a passion for exceptional craftsmanship and timeless elegance. What began as a small atelier in Jakarta has grown into an internationally recognized symbol of luxury and refinement.

For over three decades, we have maintained our commitment to creating pieces of unparalleled quality. Each item is meticulously handcrafted by our skilled artisans, many of whom have been with us for generations, passing down their expertise and dedication to excellence.

Today, Christian Wijaya continues to honor its founder's vision while embracing innovation. Our collections blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design, creating modern heirlooms that stand the test of time.

Our Headquarters

Our global headquarters in Jakarta stands as a testament to our brand's philosophy. Housed in a meticulously restored heritage building, it combines classical architecture with modern elements, reflecting our commitment to honoring tradition while embracing the future.

The building features our design studios, workshops, and a private showroom where clients can experience our collections in an intimate setting. Every aspect of our headquarters has been thoughtfully designed to inspire creativity and showcase our dedication to craftsmanship.

We welcome visitors by appointment to experience the world of Christian Wijaya firsthand and witness our artisans at work.
